Our Features

WHAT IS SAKAY?

Sakay is a contactless fare payment transaction service that allows passengers to pay fares in just one tap. It offers a seamless and convenient service for the Ikot La Salle e-jeep shuttle service in De La Salle University - Dasmariñas. This product is inspired by beep cards mostly used in LRT and MRT trains, including P2P buses, PUVs, etc.

HOW DOES SAKAY WORK?

Sakay features an automated fare collection (AFC) system designed with efficiency, convenience and security in mind for a streamlined fare collection process, ensuring a seamless experience for Lasallian passengers and transit operators. Sakay offers various payment methods, such as mainly through the use of a transit card that allows them to simply tap Sakay card to the card reader to pay their fare. With Sakay, you’ll be able to enjoy seamless traveling through card or mobile devices, ensuring a safer cash transaction from passengers to transit operators.

EXCLUSIVE FOR IKOT LASALLE

Sakay is available exclusively for DLSU-D’s Ikot La Salle service shuttle. Sakay is deeply committed to accessibility ensuring that not only the convenience of the passengers that is valued but also aims to set a new standard for inclusivity within the university’s service shuttle. The brand demonstrates its commitment by uring that the system is intuitive, easy to use, and accessible for students and employees without compromising on functionality or efficiency.
